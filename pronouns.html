Pronouns

Personal Pronouns:

Personal pronouns are pronouns that refer to people.

Singular Subjective: I, You, He, She, It

Singular Objective: Me, You, Him, Her, It

Plural Subjective: We, You, They

Plural Objective: Us, You, Them

First Person Pronouns:

I went home.

We ate the mangoes.

Second Person Pronouns:

What do you want?

You should go home.

Third Person Pronouns:

He went home.

She is not feeling well.

They gave him the job.

Possessive Pronouns:

This is mine.

The money was for all of you.

Reflexive Pronouns:

She, herself, escorted the boy.

Myself, Ourselves

Yourself, Yourselves

Subjective and Objective Case:

She came for her.

Sarah helped him.

I will get it soon.

Demonstrative Pronouns:

That is terrible.

This is mine.

Indefinite Pronouns:

Each of the boys has been fed.

Someone is always watching.

Many problems we face can be solved through dialogue.
