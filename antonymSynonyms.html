Antonyms

Examples of Antonyms:

  • Good — Bad
  • Hot — Cold
  • Happy — Sad
  • Fast — Slow
  • Big — Small
  • Strong — Weak

Antonyms are words with opposite meanings. They are used to express contrast or opposition. Learning antonyms can help improve vocabulary and understanding of language nuances.

Synonyms

Examples of Synonyms:

  • Get — Acquire
  • Run — Sprint
  • Happy — Joyful
  • Big — Large
  • Strong — Powerful
  • Fast — Quick

Synonyms are words with similar meanings that can be used interchangeably in certain contexts. They provide variety in language and can enhance writing and communication skills.
