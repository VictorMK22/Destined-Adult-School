ABOUT US

Destined Adult School is a leading platform for language learning dedicated to helping adults acquire new language skills and improve their communication abilities.

At Destined Adult School, we believe that learning a new language should be accessible, engaging, and effective. Our team of experienced language instructors and dedicated professionals is committed to providing high-quality language education to our students.

With our state-of-the-art online learning platform, students can access a wide range of language courses designed to cater to their specific needs and goals. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an advanced learner aiming to enhance your fluency, we have the right course for you.

Our courses are structured to ensure a comprehensive learning experience. Through interactive lessons, real-life examples, and practical exercises, you'll develop your language skills in a supportive and engaging environment. Our instructors provide personalized guidance and feedback to help you progress quickly and effectively.

Join us at Destined Adult School and embark on a language learning journey that will open doors to new opportunities, expand your horizons, and connect you with a global community of learners.

OUR VALUES

The Destined School core values are encapsulated by the acronym EFA i.e.

  • Equality for all.
  • Formal education for all.
  • Access to opportunities for all.

MISSION

To provide formal education opportunities for the vulnerable and marginalized regardless of their ages, social status or gender.

VISSION

The Destined School Vision is of a society in which all individual of all ages are actively involved in promoting sustainable futures.
