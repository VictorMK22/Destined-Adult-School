Tenses

Simple Past Tense:

- Play—played

- Pose—posed

- Kiss—kissed

Irregular Verbs:

- Spell—spelled or spelt

- Spit—spat

- Drive—drove

Simple Present Tense:

- I walk to school every day.

- She plays football.

- Ken is a poet.

Perfect Tense:

- He had finished the test when the teacher arrived.

- She has arrived.

- She has been eating ugali.
